How to play Multi4Connect

  • Select one of the following game modes:
    1. Play against CPU
    2. 2 players
    3. 4 players
    4. 6 players
    5. 8 players

  • The objective of the game is to place coins of your colour on the board such that they connect horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

  • The game progresses in turns. Each player must wait for their turn in order to drop a coin of their colour on the board.

  • On your turn, click anywhere in a column to drop your coin in that column. The coin occupies the lowest available space in the column.

  • The game ends when a player connects four coins. The first player to do so is declared winner.
